Biography


Joseph Ibila Kililo is a young Artist born in 1997 the Democratic Republic of Congo and is from South Kivu. He fled his country of origin in 2019 due to ethnic insecurity. He lost his father, which led to major threats to his life. He is a carpenter and sculptor.
