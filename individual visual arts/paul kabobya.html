Biography


Paul Kabobya is the father of six children. He fled his country of origin, the Democratic Republic of Congo, in December of 2014 due to war and came to seek asylum in Malawi. He currently lives with his family in the Dzaleka refugee camp where he makes and sells his art. However, there are challenges he faces. The profit he gets is very little, due to having only a few customers.
