Biography collected and written by Primo Luanda Bauma
Photo of Simeon by Primo Luanda Bauma

My name is Simeon Blaise Kijege. I’m Congolese by nationality and aged 47 years old. I’m married and a father of 7 children. I started photography in the nineties. I did a 6 months course and when I finished, I bought my first camera. Since then, I do photography. It’s my regular occupation. It has made me to be known by a lot of people. Photography is my only source of income. In 2011, I bought a DSLR camera, whereby I improved and got familiar with a digital camera. Due to insecurity and violence, I decided to flee Congo to Malawi for safety. When I reached Dzaleka, I had my old camera. I started working with it and after 2 years, I bought other photography equipment like a tripod, lenses, and a new laptop as well. I have faced so many challenges, such as doing services for people and not getting paid, as well as cell phones having to be replaced. Reduced job opportunities in photography means I no longer get as many customers as I used to get before. Lack of enough training and trainers is also a challenge for me. What I would advise is that people should learn different skills or they should join photography in order to sustain their lives.
